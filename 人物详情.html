Marc Andreesen

Marc Andreesen is the Software Engineer and Entrepreneur by profession. He is the co-founder of Mosaic, the very first web browser, and is one of the early bitcoins proponents. Marc Andreessen’s venture capital firm, Andreessen Horowitz, invested just under $50 million in Bitcoin-related start-ups. The firm is keen to find and support more Bitcoin-based investment opportunities. His famous article “Why Bitcoin Matters” was featured in New York Times as well as on other secondary mediums.
